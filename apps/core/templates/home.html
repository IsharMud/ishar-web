    Ishar is a high medieval fantasy MUD built on one idea: the world ends, and then it begins again. Each season the goddess Varenya turns the wheel — the map resets, old legends are wiped, and everyone starts from nothing. Whatever you make of yourself before the next turning is what you get.

    Six classes, each with its own way of staying alive: Warrior, Rogue, Magician, Necromancer, Cleric, Shaman. You'll fight down through catacombs, snowbound forests where angels and daemons hunt each other, and swamps that don't intend to let you back out. Combat is hack-and-slash, but the bosses reward players who think rather than players who grind.

    The community is active in-game and on Discord, and new content arrives with every season.

    Come make your mark before the wheel turns again.
