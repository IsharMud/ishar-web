Game-Icons.net icon attribution
===============================

The skill-bar icons in game-icons.svg are a curated subset (166 glyphs) of the
Game-Icons.net collection.

  Source:   https://game-icons.net  (repository: https://github.com/game-icons/icons)
  License:  Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported (CC BY 3.0)
            https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/
            License text: https://github.com/game-icons/icons/blob/master/license.txt

The icons were extracted from the Iconify redistribution of the collection
(@iconify-json/game-icons, itself CC BY 3.0) and rewritten as <symbol> elements
that recolor via CSS (fill="currentColor"). No icon artwork was altered beyond
this recoloring hook.

Per-artist credits for each glyph are listed on the icon's page at
game-icons.net. The Game-Icons.net collection is the work of many contributors
(Lorc, Delapouite, Skoll, sbed, John Colburn, Felbrigg, Carl Olsen, and others);
this attribution notice and the links above are provided in accordance with
CC BY 3.0.
